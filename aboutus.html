About Our Company

Zanko Sdn. Bhd. – Incepted in 2013 – was founded by a group of highly experienced professionals with the vision to provide world-class IT solutions. Zanko is a leading Bumiputera Application Development, Information Technology Services and Consultancy player headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia with various full- fledged offices and subsidiaries in Johor, Kelantan, Cyberjaya & Bangi, Selangor.

For the past decade, we have been developing numerous software in various industries by innovating and making effective use of latest cutting-edge technologies with more than RM200 million worth of project value.

Zanko is a recognised premium provider of comprehensive IT business solutions, providing tailored services, unique products and viable solutions in helping our clients to make a breakthrough and stay ahead in the competitive digitalisation world. We are proud to be recognised as the No. 1 Online Assessment Solution for the largest Malaysian Examination Board and other education institutions in Malaysia and, as of today we are constantly striving to exceed our own expectations.

Zanko will never stop in its journey to inspire, initiate, co-create, and lead the future change to make Malaysia a leader of digital innovation in Southeast Asia.
